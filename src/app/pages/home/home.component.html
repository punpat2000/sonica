About Sonica

Sonica was established in 2000 and has been a trusted leader in the UV screen printing industry. We are the sole distributor of Norcote International, Inc. in Thailand, bringing world-class UV screen printing solutions to manufacturers across the country.

We specialize in providing high-quality UV screen printing inks, premium squeegees, advanced LED/UV curing systems, and precision UV intensity meters. Our commitment to excellence and innovation, combined with our exclusive partnership with Norcote International, has made us a preferred partner for leading manufacturers across Thailand.

About Norcote International

For over 40 years, Norcote has focused on developing innovative, market-driven UV curable printing inks, helping our customers lead their markets. As your needs evolve, we evolve with you, ensuring our products remain cutting-edge.

Whether you're new to UV printing or a seasoned veteran, our Technical Service team is here to provide the right tools and support, from laboratory testing to on-site troubleshooting, ensuring your projects are completed successfully.

Our team of color experts offers quick and precise color matching, with a turnaround time of one to three days, and provides on-site training with the latest color measurement technology. Additionally, our friendly Customer Service Representatives ensure your orders are handled efficiently and delivered promptly, meeting your expectations every time.
